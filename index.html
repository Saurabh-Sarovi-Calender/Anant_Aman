PEOPLE HELPING PEOPLE

Anant Aman Social Welfare Society, NGO originates from Indore, is a beacon of hope committed to working with underprivileged sections of society and bringing in a relevant change. Compassionate in spirit, this organization strives to address relevant challenges with interventions such as cancer awareness programmes, educational outreach, projects for women’s empowerment, and environmental protection initiatives.

Mission Vision

Mission

Our mission is to promote education, health, and environmental awareness while uplifting underprivileged communities. We strive to ensure social security, advocate for rights, and improve living standards for marginalized groups. Through initiatives like education for poor children, health and hygiene awareness, and rights advocacy, we aim to empower lives and contribute to national progress.

Vision

Our vision is to build a compassionate society where no one suffers in silence. We aim to raise awareness about cancer, support early detection and prevention, and provide care to those in need. Through regular food distribution drives, we strive to eliminate hunger and uplift underserved communities, ensuring health, dignity, and hope for a brighter future promoting education.
